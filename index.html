With all my love

Dear Jinji,

I wanted to make something simple and beautiful for you, because I love you.

You are the soft part of my day, the thought I return to without trying, and the person who makes ordinary moments feel lit from within. Loving you has taught me that home is not only a place. It is a voice, a laugh, a hand to hold, and a heart that understands mine.

I love your tenderness, your strength, your humor, your warmth, and the little details that make you, you. I love the life we are building in quiet ways, in hopeful ways, in ways that make the future feel less far away.

Every day with you gives me another reason to believe in the kind of love that is patient. You make my world more possible, and I hope I spend my life making you feel as cherished as you make me feel.

Today, as you celebrate another year of life, I hope it brings you joy, peace, growth, and every beautiful thing your heart hopes for. More than anything, I hope it brings you countless reasons to smile.

Yours
