A letter for the one I love

For Jinji

My favorite place in the world is wherever your heart is near mine.

With all my love

Dear Jinji,

I wanted to make something simple and beautiful for you, because the way I love you is both: simple in its truth, beautiful in every way it has changed my life.

You are the soft part of my day, the thought I return to without trying, and the person who makes ordinary moments feel lit from within. Loving you has taught me that home is not only a place. It is a voice, a laugh, a hand to hold, and a heart that understands mine.

I love the way you exist in the world. I love your tenderness, your strength, your humor, your warmth, and the little details that make you unmistakably you. I love the life we are building in quiet ways, in hopeful ways, in ways that make the future feel less far away.

If this page is ever found by anyone else, let it be known plainly: Jinji is the love of my life. I am grateful for you, proud of you, and endlessly lucky to love you.

Always, and in all the ways I know how,

Yours
